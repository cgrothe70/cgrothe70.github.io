About

My Name is Chris Grothe, and I am an IT graduate

At present, I enjoy assembling and disassembling desktop computers, servers and laptops for the purpose of repair and to see how they were designed. I run my own small “datacenter” comprised of just three secondhand servers. The backbone of my datacenter is VMWare ESXi, mainly because it’s easy to use, free and played a large part in my studies. All network connections in my home have been made with cables I have terminated and tested. I have spent the last few years figuring out what it is that I want to do for the rest of my life. I am clearly interested in technology, but exactly what is it about technology that I would love to wake up each day and travel to work for. After much deliberation, I have decided that my dream job would have to be a System Administrator. The hands-on aspect gets me giddy like a child, while the ever-changing nature of technology provides me with an excuse to always remain up-to-date and familiar with the latest and greatest. A graduate of the Information Technology Support Services program at Conestoga College, I possess the skills required to fill your position. I am an ambitious and adaptable person armed with a wide range of knowledge. I have the foundational knowledge of computer hardware, Networking, Windows, Linux, VMWare vSphere, System Administration and more! I look forward to hearing back from you!
